How this chart is calculated

Damage – Total unit DPS after upgrades.
Durability – Total unit health after upgrades.
Control – Units with control traits (stuns, slows).
Mobility – Units with mobility traits.
Range – Ranged units and ranged pressure.
Economy – Miners and economy-scaling effects.

Values are normalized to show relative strengths, not exact power.
